ABOUT US

Eventus will be an association of all the college campus festivals in India. This includes both Inter or Intra level college fests. Eventus is a solution for accessing even minuscule of information about the fests. Our main motto is to connect students from campuses all over India by making them aware of Cultural, Technical, Management Fests, Workshops, Seminars organized by every college in Gandhinagar and Ahmedabad. Eventus is a platform which provides details about the fests, fests by type, fests near you and service of registration to attend or participate in an event..
